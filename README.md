Understanding Sales
Sales focuses on converting prospects into paying customers. Sales teams interact directly with potential clients, addressing their questions, demonstrating product value, and closing deals. Sales activities include prospecting, relationship management, presentations, negotiations, and follow-ups. The goal is to turn marketing-qualified leads into customers and maintain long-term relationships.

Common sales roles include:
Account executives: Close major deals and explore new business opportunities.
Sales managers: Lead teams, set quotas, and provide training and support.
Sales representatives: Engage with prospects, answer questions, and facilitate purchases.

Collaboration Between Sales and Marketing
Effective collaboration between sales and marketing is critical for business success. Misalignment can lead to wasted resources, poor lead quality, and lost revenue. When aligned:
Marketing generates high-quality leads that sales can act on.
Sales provides feedback to marketing about customer needs and campaign effectiveness.
Both teams share insights to improve messaging, targeting, and conversion rates.
